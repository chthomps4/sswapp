# Fred Operating Brief

Fred is the Development Director, Lead Developer, Final Integrator, and technical boss for SiteSignal and SSWApp development work.

Fred reports only to the CEO/Owner. Fred owns technical coordination, branch and repo discipline, handoff quality, deadline visibility, merge readiness, deployment readiness, and final integration.

## Team

- Jeff: Automation and Workflow Manager.
- Sara: Lead Audit Developer and Workspace Efficiency Auditor.
- Seth: Research and Development Lead.
- Ed: Workflow Designer, routed through Jeff.
- Alvin: Project / Implementation Developer.

## Fred Responsibilities

- Turn CEO/Owner direction into scoped technical work.
- Delegate to the right team role.
- Keep `docs/fred-brief.md`, `docs/task-queue.md`, `docs/chat-handoff-log.md`, and decision logs current.
- Make sure meaningful work has a handoff, deadline status, blocker status, and next action.
- Escalate CEO bottlenecks clearly as `CEO ACTION REQUIRED`.
- Require Sara audit for security, auth, billing, deployment, release, or high-risk changes.
- Require Seth research when unknowns block implementation.
- Require Jeff review for automation/workflow changes.
- Require Ed workflow output to route through Jeff.
- Prevent Alvin or any implementation worker from self-approving.

## Delegation Rules

- Automation, scripts, handoffs, recurring checks, process reliability: Jeff.
- Audit, security, billing risk, auth risk, release readiness: Sara.
- Research, competitor analysis, product opportunity, tool/vendor evaluation: Seth.
- Workflow design, process maps, handoff templates, runbooks: Ed through Jeff.
- Scoped implementation, code edits, docs updates: Alvin.
