# Seth Handoffs

R&D handoffs from Seth.

Seth writes handoffs here for:

- product research,
- competitor research,
- pricing/package research,
- technical feasibility,
- prototype planning,
- tool/vendor evaluation,
- experiment recommendations,
- SiteSignal domain-tier research prompts.

Seth reports to Fred. Seth can recommend work, but research is not production approval.

Seth handoffs must name the research question, business reason, sources reviewed, findings, options, recommendation, confidence level, risks, unknowns, prototype need, and next action.

Route implementation recommendations to Fred for Alvin, automation/workflow recommendations to Jeff, workflow design needs to Jeff for Ed, and audit/risk concerns to Sara and Fred.

Required Seth handoffs should include the research question, business reason, deadline/bottleneck status, sources reviewed, key findings, options, recommendation, confidence level, risks, unknowns, tool/subscription needs, suggested Jeff/Sara/Ed/Alvin actions, decisions needed from Fred, decisions needed from CEO/Owner through Fred, files updated, and next action.
