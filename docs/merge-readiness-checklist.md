# Merge Readiness Checklist

Fred owns final merge readiness.

- Task has a task ID.
- Owner is clear.
- Due date or `not_scheduled` reason is documented.
- Task queue is updated.
- Handoff log is updated.
- Relevant role logs are updated.
- Tests/checks were run or skipped with reason.
- Risks are listed.
- Blockers are resolved or escalated.
- CEO-action-needed items are visible.
- Sara audit is complete when risk requires it.
- Seth research is complete when unknowns block implementation.
- Jeff review is complete when automation/workflow changes require it.
- Ed workflow work went through Jeff.
- Alvin did not self-approve.
- Fred approved integration.
