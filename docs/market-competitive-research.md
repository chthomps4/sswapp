## Research Fields

For each competitor or market opportunity, document:

- Competitor / alternative name.
- Pricing.
- Positioning.
- Customer segment.
- Feature differences.
- Gaps SiteSignal can exploit.
- SEO/content opportunity.
- Product risks.
- Minimum viable feature recommendation.
- Free/Pro/Agency tier impact.
