## Tool Approval Rule

If a tool, vendor, subscription, paid API, domain, hosting plan, monitoring plan, design tool, or external service is needed, Seth researches it and Fred routes approval to the CEO/Owner.

## AI Workflow Service Tool Boundary

The internal business hub can demonstrate a review-safe AI workflow stack, but live integrations for client demos must stay manual-first until approved. Do not configure paid plans, external automations, publishing, deployment, billing, auth, or production data access from the service draft alone.
